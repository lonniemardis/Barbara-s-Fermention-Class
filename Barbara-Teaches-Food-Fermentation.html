Why ferment?

Fermented foods brighten meals, support digestion, and preserve produce naturally. Barbara's approach is practical, low-stress, and designed for home cooks who want real results without mystery.

What you'll learn

Basic fermentation

Understand salt ratios, fermentation times, and how to create crisp, tangy vegetables with confidence.

Flavor building

Mix spices, aromatics, and seasonal produce to craft sauerkrauts, kimchis, and pickled blends that taste amazing.

Home kitchen setup

Learn what tools you really need, how to manage jars, and ways to keep your fermenting foods safe and delicious.
